About EZ360

EZ360 was created to make showcasing 360° video on events hassle free and has since evolved to a complete Content Management System that makes the management, distribution and playback of Virtual Reality content as easy as possible.

Many businesses that are working with VR are creating VR content or solutions for others to create VR content. Instead of focussing on the creation of the content itself, our focus lies on enabling organizations with their own VR content to successfully apply this in practice. We differentiate ourselves from our competitors by focusing on ease of use. We make it easy for organisations to use Virtual Reality in a scalable way. We have a product that is being used by organisations around the world. We are continuously improving the product and adding new features and support for different type of headsets.
