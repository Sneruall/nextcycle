The Innovation Cycle

Innovation is the process of creating better solutions to meet new challenges. This process starts with an idea, but involves way more than that. Think about prototyping, networking, planning, developing a business model, implementation and deployment. And it's not a race to the finishline, it is an iterative process which requires constant attention. Are you ready for the Innovation Cycle?
