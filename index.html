The Innovation Cycle

Innovation is not a race to the finishline. It is an iterative process which requires constant attention. Innovation is never a straight line, but always requires you to jump into the next phase. This asks for accurate planning, monitoring progress, taking action and adding value. In the end, it's not about ideas, it is about making ideas happen. Are you ready for the Next Innovation Cycle?

The Investment Cycle

Getting funded is crucial for a startup, it is the fuel for taking the next steps. On the other side, the startup also takes onboard a new co-pilot, therefore it is crucial to have a good fit between the startup and investor. However, it can be challenging for a startup, but also investor, to find the right match. Make connections, have a good match and get funded. Are you ready for the Next Investment Cycle?
